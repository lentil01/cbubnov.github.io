    Eximchain

    Lead UI/UX Designer

    May 2018 - May 2019

    Eximchain is a Singapore-based company building blockchain applications for the supply chain space. Supply chains are global by nature; to ensure the legal compliance and integrity of our network, a comprehensive identity validation process was implemented.

    After user testing many prototypes, we released a mobile app to securely create a wallet, validate user identities, and view network members. A complementary desktop app was created to send and sign transactions, view token balances, and interact with smart contracts. By balancing ease of use with security through the native mobile app and desktop app combination, we increased our member network base and provided a trusted system to them.
