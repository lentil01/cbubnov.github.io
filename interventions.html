    • Architecting the first student-led design conference in an accessible and impactful way.

    I nterventions is an annual conference in its third year that takes place in April at Northeastern University.

    The conference is comprised of speakers, panels, and workshops, and discusses the role of creative thinkers in developing brands, products, and services in an age of rapid technological advancement. There are appearances from companies such as Github, Dribbble, Airbnb, and Netflix.

    I led a team of five talented people to create the concept and identity of the conference, and assure its continuation as an annual event.

    Idea

    So um... how do we do this?

    This type of project was a first for Scout Studio and all members involved. We spent a lot of time brainstorming ideas, names, concepts, and visual directions. Our soul searching turned into rapid-fire visuals, keeping in mind the question: How does design intervene in the world, for better or worse?

    ↑ A branding exercise we did with the client. After each person completed the exercise, we combined all answers to yield a collaborative preliminary concept of the conference.

    ↑ Brainstorming symbolism through three important parts of the conference theme: Interventions, Scout, and Society.

    ↑ We created user flows for the site manager, speakers, attendees, and interested parties to narrow down components for an MVP.

    ↑ Some directions that didn't make the cut.

    Identity

    We settled on a direction that was loud, bold, and full of hands. Hands are a great tool to show the power of intervention in a common way, and make great wayfinding arrows. We put together a guide on how to create assets, a messaging guide for the voice of the conference, posters, and wayfinding for future teams to base their designs off of.

    ↑ Our primary typefaces are Rubik and Sentinel Italic. The color palette was chosen to exude a fun, dynamic atmosphere - the conference is a place with an energetic, purposeful, and contagious vibe. For a more detailed look at the Interventions identity, check out our brand guidelines.

    Website

    We put up an introductory website and planned for its various states - before, during, and after the conference. You can doodle on the site with your cursor and buy tickets.

    ↑ In keeping with the spirit of the theme, we made the site interactive so people could directly intervene and draw their own designs on the landing page.

    tools used: pen & paper, illustrator, sketch, photoshop, sticky notes
