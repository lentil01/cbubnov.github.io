    Done At

    Seacor provides sea-based transportation and logistics services across a wide array of industries.

    The company came to us with a broad ask - to transform from a tug-leasing company with tech into a tech company that leases tugs. We built a product that interprets ships' radio signals as common port call events, and provides analytics on this normally manual process. This product has enormous potential in speeding up port calls, reducing human error, and improving the sustainability of the shipping industry.
