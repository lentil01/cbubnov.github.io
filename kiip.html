    • Kiip - Vital Document Management

    Simple, secure document management for vulnerable populations seeking affordable housing.

    Role

    Design + Direction

    Year

    2020 - 2021

    Done At

    Two Bulls

    Kiip connects people who need affordable housing and their case workers with government agencies.

    Whether it's a background check, unaffordable prices, or the lack of a credit score, one of the most common struggles of vulnerable populations is that they are unable to find shelter. What started out as an open-source project for the city of Baltimore turned into a full-fledged product that streamlines the complex process of coordinating with family members, case workers, and government agencies to become eligible for subsidized housing programs.
