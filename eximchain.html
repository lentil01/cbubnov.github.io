    Eximchain is a Singapore-based company building blockchain applications for the supply chain space.

    We built at a simple mobile app to securely create a wallet, validate user identities, and view network members. A complementary desktop app was created to send and sign transactions, view token balances, and interact with smart contracts. By balancing ease of use with security through the native mobile app and desktop app combination, we increased our member network base and provided a trusted system to them. A comprehensive identity validation process was implemented to ensure the legal compliance and integrity of the network.
